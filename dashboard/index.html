The Story Behind the Data

The Business Question

Where should Starbucks expand next to capture the next 50 million customers?

With North American and European markets nearing saturation, the next phase of hyper-growth lies in unserved megacities in the Global South. This analysis identifies specific locations where population density, economic trajectory, and lack of competition converge.

Methodology

We cross-referenced the World Cities Database (14,000+ cities) against the complete Starbucks Global Store Directory. By filtering for populations >1M and store count == 0, we utilized a "gap analysis" technique to isolate high-value "Starbucks" opportunities.
